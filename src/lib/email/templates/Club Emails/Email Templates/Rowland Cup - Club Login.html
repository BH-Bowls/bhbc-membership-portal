Logging In — Quick Guide

  1. Go to portal.burgesshillbowlsclub.com/clublogin or follow the link on our website.
  2. Enter the Username and Password shown above.
  3. You will be prompted to choose a new password — pick something memorable and keep it safe. All your club's Rowland contacts can use the same login.
  4. Once in, go to Rowland Cup to see your draw and Clubs to find your section contacts.

What You Can Do in the Portal

  • Your draw — see all your matches, opponents, and play-by dates for the competition.
  • Club contacts — find the name, phone, and email of the contact at each club in your section.
  • Submit results — enter your match result and player names once the game has been played. You can also attach a photo of the score card — very easy on a smartphone.
  • Competition progress — see the latest results across all matches in your section.
  • Edit your club details — update your contact details, or add your skips so other clubs can reach the right person to arrange matches.
  • Help — comprehensive guidance is built in to the portal. Have a look once you are logged in.

A Quick Favour — Check Your Club Details

The portal lists contact details for every club in the competition, so that clubs can get in touch to arrange games. It is worth taking a moment to check that your own club's details are correct — name, phone number, email address, and the right person to contact for arranging matches.

Once you are logged in you can update these yourself — go to Clubs, find your club, and tap Edit. Alternatively, if you would rather we do it, just let us know at burgesshillbc@gmail.com and we will get it sorted.

Having trouble logging in? If you have any difficulty, please contact us at burgesshillbc@gmail.com and we will get you sorted.

We hope the portal makes it easier to keep track of your fixtures and get in touch with opponents. Good luck in the Rowland Cup this season!

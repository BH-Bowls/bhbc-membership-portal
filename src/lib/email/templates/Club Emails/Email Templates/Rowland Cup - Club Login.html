This year, Burgess Hill Bowls Club is introducing an online portal for all Rowland Cup clubs. The portal gives you quick access to your competition draw, match schedule, and the ability to submit results — all in one place. You can view the draw and enter results for any Rowland Cup section your club is entered in.

If you ever have difficulty using the portal, you can always continue to send results to the organisers in the usual way — the portal is there to help, not to get in the way.

We still need to see the score card. When you enter your results through the portal, if you are using your phone, you can take a photo of the score card at the same time — this will be emailed to the Rowland Cup organisers along with the result. Alternatively, you can submit it in the usual way.

What You Can Do in the Portal

  • Your draw — see all your matches, opponents, and play-by dates for the competition.
  • Club contacts — find the name, phone, and email of the contact at each club in your section.
  • Submit results — enter your match result and player names once the game has been played.
  • Competition progress — see the latest results across all matches in your section.

Logging In — Quick Guide

  1. Go to portal.burgesshillbowlsclub.com/clublogin or follow the link on our website.
  2. Enter the Username and Password shown above.
  3. You will be prompted to choose a new password — pick something memorable and keep it safe. All your club's Rowland contacts can use the same login.
  4. Once in, go to Rowland Cup to see your draw and Contacts to find your section contacts.

A Quick Favour — Check Your Club Details

The portal lists contact details for every club in the competition, so that clubs can get in touch with each other to arrange games. While you are logged in, it is worth taking a moment to check that your own club's details are correct — name, phone number, and email address.

If anything is wrong or out of date, please let us know at burgesshillbc@gmail.com and we will get it updated.

Having trouble logging in? If you have any difficulty, please contact us at burgesshillbc@gmail.com and we will get you sorted.

We hope the portal makes it easier to keep track of your fixtures and get in touch with opponents. Good luck in the Rowland Cup this season!

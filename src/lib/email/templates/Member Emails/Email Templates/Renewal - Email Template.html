Payment Options

We kindly ask that you pay by Bank Transfer if possible. This is the most efficient method for the club.

  1. Bank Transfer: Preferred method (zero fees for the club).
  2. Card Payments: Accepted at the bar, but incur bank charges and extra admin work.
  3. Cash & Cheque: High workload and fees; only accepted in exceptional circumstances.

The Buddy System:
To make things as easy as possible, I have already pre-linked the accounts for most married couples. This means you can simply log in as yourself and "switch" to your spouse's account to renew for them.

To switch accounts: Click the menu/profile icon and select "Switch User". Once finished, select "Exit Switch" from the same menu.

To link a new Buddy: If you want to manage someone else who isn't already linked, YOUR User ID (e.g. firstname.lastname) must be entered into THEIR Profile in the "Buddy User ID" field to grant you permission.
